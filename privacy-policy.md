# Privacy Policy for Nixy

This privacy policy describes how Nixy (the "app") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use the app.

## What personal information do we collect?

The app allows you to take notes using Nextcloud as a backend. To do this, we need to collect and store the following information, from your Nextcloud account:

-  Username
    • Password
    • Token

Those information are not stored in our servers.

## How do we protect your personal information?

We store your personal information securely on your device and encrypt it when it is transmitted to Nextcloud. We do not store any of your personal information on our servers.

## How can you control your personal information?

You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by logging into your Nextcloud account. You can also uninstall the app from your device to remove all of your personal information from the app.

## What third-party services do we use?
1We use Sentry as a third-party service to collect logs about your device if you consent to it. This helps us to improve the app and fix any bugs or errors. Sentry's privacy policy can be found here: https://sentry.io/privacy/

You can opt out of sending logs to Sentry at any time by changing the settings in the app.

## How do we update this privacy policy?

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting a notice on the app or by email if you have provided one. Your continued use of the app after such notice constitutes your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at nixy@nixy.com.
